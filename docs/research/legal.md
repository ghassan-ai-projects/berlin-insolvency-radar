## Data Protection (GDPR / BDSG)

### Corporate vs Personal Data
| Data Type | Risk Level | Notes |
|-----------|-----------|-------|
| Company name, register number, sector | 🟢 Low | Public register data, business information |
| Financial figures (revenue, employees) | 🟢 Low | Public if from register filings |
| Individual/consumer insolvencies | 🔴 High | Personal data — GDPR restricts commercial reuse |
| Names of Geschäftsführer / directors | 🟡 Medium | May be public in register; check context |
| Insolvency administrator contact | 🟢 Low | Professional contact info |

**Decision:** Skip consumer insolvencies. Focus exclusively on corporate filings (GmbH, AG, UG, KG, OHG). This eliminates the most significant GDPR exposure.

### Key Rules
1. **Public accessibility ≠ unrestricted reuse** — The official register is public, but commercial reuse of personal data requires legal basis
2. **Purpose limitation** — Data must be used for stated, legitimate purposes
3. **Data minimization** — Only collect what's needed
4. **Deletion obligation** — Insolvency data is removed from the public register 6 months after proceedings conclude

## German Press Law (Pressekodex)

If the newsletter is framed as business journalism / financial press:
- Press privilege may provide a legitimate interest basis for processing
- Requires editorial independence, journalistic standards
- May reduce GDPR exposure but not eliminate it

**Recommendation:** Frame the newsletter as business intelligence / financial journalism, not direct marketing.

---

## Commercial Communications (UWG)

- Sending unsolicited marketing emails to consumers is illegal without explicit consent
- B2B cold outreach has more leeway but still regulated
- **Solution:** Use permission-based subscription (opt-in) via newsletter platform — standard practice

## Other Risks

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Defamation | Present facts, attribute sources, avoid subjective claims without evidence |
| Financial advice regulation | Add prominent disclaimer: "Not investment or financial advice. For informational purposes only." |
| Insider trading (if applicable) | Information is public (register data) — not insider information |
| Data accuracy | Disclaim that data is sourced from public registers and may contain errors |
